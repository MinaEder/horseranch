About River Ranch


Howdy & Welcome

River Ranch is excited to share our services with you at Texas Horse Park! We have a little of something for everyone. We offer horseback riding trail rides and lessons, kid's parties, adult parties, corporate events, weddings, and intimate gatherings of all kinds. And our special events are sure to please!

Also, as a 501(c)3 non-profit, we not only provide fantastic services to the general public but we give back by providing services for underprivileged children, at-risk youth,, those with special needs, senior citizens and veterans.

Our Activities

Riding Lessons

At River Ranch, we strongly believe that all riders benefit from having a strong foundation with an emphasis on Horsemanship. All students will learn the basics of horseback riding. Great lesson packages are offered year round at an affordable rate for everyone 7 years of age or older and in good health. ​ Lessons are structured according to each individual student. We teach the students all aspects of horsemanship from grooming, saddling, basic care to a good, strong foundation of horseback riding skills.


Trail Rides in 2021

Get off the beaten path and explore the hidden beauties within the Trinity Forest by horseback! River Ranch has it all when it comes to trail ridin' at the Texas Horse Park. Let us saddle up a horse just for you and then get ready to take an awesome ride. No experience required.


Scout Badges

Our Horsemanship Badge can be earned in a two hour lesson. The first hour is focused on learning about the basics of a horse’s body parts, learning each part of the tack used in horseback riding and explaining its usage, learning how to tack and how to saddle a horse. We also talk about a horse’s daily routine, First Aid and how to properly care for a horse which includes showing the Scouts how to properly “muck out a horse or pony pen and stall” and discussing basic nutrition.


School and Group Tours

Come meet the River Ranch Family of Animals, explore the Texas Horse Park and learn about the Cowboy World. Time will be provided between activities for guests to enjoy a sack lunch. Larger groups will be divided into smaller groups & rotate through the stations.
